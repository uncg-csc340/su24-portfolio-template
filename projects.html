Dyvert

The goal of the DyVert web application is to connect users to new media content and to provide an interactive method of bookmarking interesting content. Content Creators create content cards describing the media, genre, synopsis, and other details. Users are presented with the content cards, and they either swipe right to add the card to their collection or left to discard the card. Users can filter content cards shown to them based on media type and/or genre. Users can also add reviews for any media that is part of their collection. Admins are responsible for content moderation and membership.
